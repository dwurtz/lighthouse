Common questions

General

What is Déjà?

Déjà feeds your email, calendar, messages, and meetings into Claude, Cursor, and the AI tools you already use. It builds a local knowledge base of your people, projects, and commitments.

Who is Déjà for?

Anyone who uses AI tools and wants them to have full context about their work. Developers, founders, PMs, and teams.

What AI tools does it work with?

Claude, Cursor, Windsurf, ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot. Any tool that supports MCP or can read local files.

What platforms does it support?

macOS 14+ (Sonoma and later), Apple Silicon and Intel.

Privacy & Data

Where does my data go?

Everything stays on your machine. Your knowledge base is a local folder you can open, back up, or delete anytime. Nothing is sent to a server.

Does Déjà read my emails?

Déjà syncs your Gmail metadata and content to build your local knowledge base. The data never leaves your machine.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Your knowledge base is a regular folder on your Mac. Delete it anytime.

Setup

How long does setup take?

About 5 minutes. Download, sign in with Google, and Déjà catches up on your recent history.

What does the onboarding process look like?

On first launch, Déjà ingests your recent email, calendar, and contacts. It builds a knowledge base of your people, projects, and commitments.

Do I need to configure anything?

Déjà works out of the box with Claude and Cursor. For other tools, you point them at your local knowledge base folder.

Pricing

Is it free?

Yes, Déjà is free during the beta.

Will there be paid plans?

Yes. We plan to offer Pro and Enterprise tiers with additional integrations and team features.

How do I sign up for the beta?

Download the app from our download page. No waitlist.
