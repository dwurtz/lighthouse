How it works

Three steps. No ongoing effort.

1

Install

One command. Connects to your email, calendar, and messages — and adds itself to every AI tool on your Mac.

2

It catches up

Reads your last 30 days and builds a complete picture of your people and projects in minutes.

3

It keeps going

Every new email, message, and meeting makes your AI smarter. Automatically, forever.

Try it in sixty seconds

Free, open source, runs entirely on your Mac.

$ git clone https://github.com/dwurtz/dejaa
$ cd dejaa && python -m dejaa configure
$ python -m dejaa monitor

# That's it. Open Claude and ask about your day.
