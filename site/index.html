the problem

You know this. You've seen this before.

The name you can't place. The commitment buried in last week's email. The meeting notes you never wrote up. The project that quietly stalled. It's all there — scattered across Gmail, iMessage, WhatsApp, Granola, Google Calendar, and a dozen tabs. Déjà watches all of it and remembers so you don't have to.

what it does

Your life, continuously remembered

personal wiki

People and projects, always current

A page for every person who matters and every project you're managing. Updated automatically from your signals. Browse in Obsidian, search from the command line, or let your AI read it.

ambient observation

It watches so you don't have to

Email threads, iMessage and WhatsApp conversations, Google Meet transcripts, Granola meeting notes, calendar events, browser activity, screenshots. All collected silently, processed locally.

meeting recording

Replaces Granola. Zero cost.

A floating pill appears before each meeting. Click "Take notes" — ScreenCaptureKit captures both sides of the call, you jot notes in a scratchpad, and AI generates a wiki event page with summary, action items, your notes, and cleaned-up transcript. Works for any call, not just calendar events.

contact enrichment

Phone, email, company — filled in

Cross-references macOS Contacts, Gmail headers, and message handles to populate every person's page with real contact info. No manual data entry.

instant onboarding

30 days of context on first run

Don't start from an empty wiki. On first launch, Déjà ingests your last month of sent email, messages, meetings, and notes — and builds a complete picture of your life in minutes.

MCP + Obsidian

Supercharges your existing AI

Installs as an MCP server on Claude, Cursor, Windsurf, and any MCP-compatible client. One tool call gives your AI full personal context. Also works as a plain Obsidian vault you can browse and edit yourself.

how it works

Observe. Remember. Serve.

👁

Observe

Gmail, Calendar, iMessage, WhatsApp, Granola, Meet transcripts, Chrome, screenshots, clipboard

⚡

Integrate

Every few minutes, new observations are analyzed and merged into the wiki — updating people pages, enriching projects, and linking entities as your day unfolds.

🔮

Reflect

Three times a day, a deeper pass reads the whole wiki, cleans up stale facts, enriches contacts, and surfaces what stands out.

Serve

Browse your wiki in Obsidian. Query it via MCP from any AI. Search from the command line. Plain markdown files — yours forever.

Up and running in sixty seconds

Clone, configure, launch. Déjà onboards itself from your last 30 days of activity.

# install
$ git clone https://github.com/dwurtz/deja
$ cd deja && python -m deja configure

# configure walks you through:
# → API key (stored in macOS Keychain)
# → identity self-page
# → wiki layout + default prompts
# → health check

# start the agent
$ python -m deja monitor

# your wiki populates in minutes.
# open ~/Deja/ in Obsidian to browse.
